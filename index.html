Welcome to Cricket

Game Description

Cricket is a bat-and-ball game played between two teams of eleven players. It is a sport that originated in England, but is now popular in many countries around the world. The objective of the game is to score more runs than the opposing team by hitting the ball and running between two sets of three wooden posts called wickets.

Game Features

1. Cricket can be played in a variety of formats including Test Matches, One Day Internationals, and Twenty20.

2. It is a sport that requires strategy, skill, and athleticism from players.

3. The sport has a rich history and culture, with passionate fans and rivalries between countries.

4. Cricket is a great way to socialize and connect with others who share a love for the game.
